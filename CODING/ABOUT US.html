ABOUT US
Our journey began with one humble shop in chennai
and grew to over one thousand locations throughout
India under the inspiring vision of our founder, cakezone
in the early 2022. Since then, our family has combined
their years of experience with the vision of a community
of collaborative shop owners, suppliers and other
partners working together to create a brand name
in the market to grow their business to what it is today.
In order to achieve this, the family offers franchises to
budding entrepreneurs & distributors partnering with
them to ensure success and excellence.
